DIY Cocktail!
  A runner type mobile hyper casual game created by Unity and tested by Voodoo. You need to pass through from the right multiplayers so that you can increase your stacks which allows you to get strenght. By using your stack power, push your enemy and beat the game!
Project Date
November 2021
The Challenge
  Since I am studying while making games, it was hard to both develop the game and study for my courses. Other than that I learned how to make random skybox color. Also to make multipliers transparent while using gradient, I used custom shader.
Project Role
This was an indie game so I had done 2D Design, 3D Design, Game Design, Developer, Video Creation
My Approach
I tried to use the typical features of a hyper casual game so that the risk I took was less than my creative games. I used multipliers to increase stacks. If you increase your stack you become more powerful and that will help you to push your enemy. If you have relativly higher stack than your enemy, you push your enemy with more speed.
