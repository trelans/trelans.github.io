Reach To Space
  I made this game for a startup company : OpenMyNetwork. The purpose was to teach children math. This game allows children to make simple math calculations while they are traveling the space. You need to get fuel to not to loose the game. In order to get fuel you need to both collect and solve the multiple choice math question. Also there are obstacles, you need to escape them by using left or right arrow keys on keyboard.
Project Date
June 2021
The Challenge
  This was my first own designed Unity 2D game. There were different people from different countries in the company. That is why I had to understand different accents. Presenting the game was also challanging and funny.
Project Role
Game Design, Game Development
My Approach
  I tried to make web-based game which can help children to do calculations.
